About Okra Maturity

Mature Okra

Mature okra pods are firm, bright green, and about 2-4 inches long. They have a tender texture and are ideal for cooking.

Over-matured Okra

Over-matured okra pods are larger, tougher, and may have a woody texture. They are often darker in color and may have visible ridges.

How It Works

This classifier uses a deep learning model based on VGG19 architecture trained on hundreds of okra images to accurately determine maturity.
